Thomson Reuters Mobility Project

In 2014 Thomson Reuters were ramping up their mobile efforts to remain competitive. Back then many places were still rocking kludgy unresponsive design with slow mobile performance. They brought me in to help adapt their websites and products.

Case study

01

Big Red Sky

This was a web based recruitment tool for managing onboarding. When I first arrived, the app's design looked like it hadn't been touched since the mid naughties. I coded various prototypes to suggest more modern takes.

02

Checkpoint

Designing for TR's flagship tax/accounting app was a big deal to many people. The userbase was huge and very familiar with old routines. I worked hard to preserve that muscle memory with the redesign.

03

Westlaw

By this stage things were really cooking. Having learned from Checkpoint I was ready for Westlaw; one of the most popular legal resources in the world. I admit, I was giddy when I heard it referenced on Better Call Saul.
